I Have Always Loved Film

Movies were always a big part of my life. Growing up, I rarely watched movies or TV on school nights. Sunday-Thursday evenings were dedicated to school and extracurricular activities, which meant that my brother and I attempted to binge on the weekends. We would watch our movies and go to blockbuster and rent new releases as often as we could. I was lucky that my dad allowed me to explore classic Action movies that were a bit out of my MPAA rating (It was First Blood and Terminator, nothing crazy). This opened up the world of, an art-form that is made up of every other art-form. On this site, I will share a random collection of some noteworthy films I have re-watched recently. I also have a page where you can suggest movies to me!
