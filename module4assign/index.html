Background

On my last site, I talked about language and some cases of mutual intelligibility. This site, while also being about language, will be about how different parts of the world mimic animal noises. I first noticed this whenever my grandmother would tell me stories with baby chick noises and I found it strange that at school, people would say "tweet tweet" for chick noises, but my family would say "pee-oh, pee-oh".
