Rehearsals and Performances

In the weeks, and sometimes days, leading up to a concert a group will meet to rehearse. This ensures everyone is prepared to play the music to the best of their ability.

Practice

In the "performance hierarchy" you are always performing, but the audience is always different. When you practice, the audience is you alone. This is where you will make the most mistakes, but also the most progress.

Rehearsal

The audience in this stage are your directors and fellow musicians. Everyone will adjust and guide each other to work together, This is where Harmony is made.

Performance

At the highest point, the audience will be the people who are... in the audience! These listeners have made an effort to see the group play. At this point, there no more mulligans and redoes. Whatever sounds come from the group will create the music that everyone can enjoy.
