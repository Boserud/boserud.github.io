Reading Music

Learning to read music is one of the first things most people learn in public school in Texas. I have always been interested in language (Foreshadowing to module3assign) and reading music was just another language to learn for me!

Fun Fact

The written "language" of music is considered a semasiographic. This means that the "words" you see have no relation to the sounds you hear.
